Support

Thank you for being a Trip Planner Plus customer! We look forward to assisting you.

In order to submit a support inquiry or feature request, please email admin@brightwoodapps.com, and we will be glad to help.

To create a new trip, go to the "My Trips" section and click the "Add New Trip" button. Fill in the trip details and save.

Planning group trips is easy with Trip Planner Plus! You can share your itinerary with friends and family by clicking the "Share" button on your trip page. This allows everyone to view, add, and adjust plans, making it simple to coordinate schedules and ideas. You can collaborate with as many people as you'd like, ensuring everyone stays updated and involved. Note that only the trip owner can delete the trip or its plans, keeping the main itinerary secure and organized.

Storing your travel documents in the app keeps everything you need right at your fingertips, even offline. Simply go to the "Documents" section of your trip and click "Upload Document" to add files like tickets, reservation details, or important travel info. You can also attach documents directly to each specific plan (like flights or hotel stays) for quick access. This way, you'll always have critical information handy without searching through emails or paper copies.

The packing list feature is designed to take the stress out of preparing for your trip. Easily add items you need to pack, organized by category (like clothes, toiletries, and electronics) so nothing gets left behind. As you pack, you can check off items just like a to-do list, helping you stay organized and ensuring you're fully prepared for your journey.
