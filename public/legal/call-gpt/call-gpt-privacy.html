Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Call GPT ("we", "our", or "us") is a service operated by Brightwood Apps that allows users to initiate real phone calls via an AI-powered voice agent in order to request, schedule, or confirm appointments with third-party businesses. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using Call GPT, you acknowledge and agree that you are authorizing our system to place phone calls on your behalf and to process information you voluntarily provide for that purpose.

We collect information that you voluntarily submit when using Call GPT, including but not limited to:

  • Appointment details: Business names, locations, requested services, preferred dates and times, and any notes or instructions you choose to provide.
  • Identity references: Your name or preferred name, which may be used by the voice agent solely to indicate that a booking is being made on your behalf.
  • Contact information: Phone numbers or other contact details necessary to complete or confirm an appointment.

You are responsible for ensuring that the information you provide is accurate, lawful, and that you have the right to share it.

Calls placed through Call GPT occur between our automated voice agent and third-party businesses. We may collect and store:

These recordings and related data may be used for service operation, quality assurance, debugging, analytics, safety monitoring, and improving system performance.

You acknowledge that businesses you call may also record calls under their own policies and applicable laws. We are not responsible for the data practices of those third parties.

  • To detect abuse, fraud, or misuse of the service
