Privacy Policy

At Recent Contacts, we take your privacy seriously. Learn how we handle and protect your contact information.

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at admin@brightwoodapps.com.

1. Contact Information Collection

Recent Contacts requires access to your device's contacts to function. Here's how we handle your data:

  • Contact Access: We only read your contacts list to identify and display recently added contacts.
  • Location Data: If you choose to enable location tagging, we store location information locally on your device.
  • Notes and Tags: Any notes or tags you add to contacts are stored locally on your device.

2. Data Storage and Security

All contact information, notes, and location data remain on your device. We do not upload or store your contacts on our servers.

The app uses your device's built-in security features to protect your data.

3. Contact Permissions

Recent Contacts requires permission to:

  • Read your contacts list
  • Access location services (optional)
  • Store notes and tags locally

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

4. Data Usage

We use your contact information solely to:

  • Display your recently added contacts
  • Show when contacts were added
  • Associate location data and notes (if you choose to add them)

5. Third-Party Access

We do not share your contacts or any associated data with third parties. All information remains private and local to your device.

6. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app. This data never includes personal information or contact details.

7. Children's Privacy

Recent Contacts is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data (all local on your device)
  • Delete your data through the app settings
  • Disable location tracking
  • Remove the app and all associated data

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be communicated through the app and on our website.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at admin@brightwoodapps.com.
