Privacy Policy

At Clip It, we prioritize your privacy. Our policy explains what data we collect and how we use it to enhance your experience.

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at admin@brightwoodapps.com.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Clip It collects only the necessary data to provide and improve the service:

  • Clip Data: We store your saved highlight clips to allow easy sharing and editing.
  • Device Information: We may collect anonymized data on device type and app interactions to optimize performance.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your email and preferences to personalize your experience.

2. Video and Storage Permissions

Clip It requires access to your device's camera roll to save and retrieve clips. We do not share or access any media beyond the clips you create within the app.

3. Sharing and Security

Your clips remain private unless you choose to share them. You can send clips via social media, messaging apps, or cloud storage services.

We do not sell or distribute any personal data. Security measures are in place to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Log Data

Like most apps, we collect log data, including device type, app usage statistics, and errors. This helps us troubleshoot and improve performance.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Clip It does not use cookies within the app. However, our website may use cookies to improve user experience and analyze traffic.

6. Third-Party Integrations

If you connect Clip It to cloud storage or social media platforms, their privacy policies will apply when sharing your clips. We recommend reviewing their terms.

7. Children's Privacy

Clip It is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated within the app.

9. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data or modify your preferences by contacting us at admin@brightwoodapps.com.
