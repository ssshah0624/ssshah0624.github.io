Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new beverage?

Simply tap the "+" button on the home screen and select your beverage type. You can choose from common options or create custom entries with specific caffeine content.

How accurate is the caffeine tracking?

Our database includes accurate caffeine content for thousands of common beverages. For custom drinks, you can input specific amounts or use our smart estimation feature based on brewing method and serving size.

Can I set custom alerts?

Yes! Go to Settings > Alerts to customize your daily caffeine limit and set up smart notifications. You can also set specific times for reminders and warnings.

How does sleep tracking work?

Decaf integrates with Apple Health to analyze your sleep patterns alongside your caffeine intake, helping you understand the relationship between your consumption and sleep quality.
