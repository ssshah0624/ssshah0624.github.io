Frequently Asked Questions

How does Recent Contacts work?

Recent Contacts automatically tracks new additions to your phone's contacts, displaying them in chronological order with timestamps and optional location data.

Can I add context about where I met someone?

Yes! You can add custom tags, location information, and detailed notes for any contact, making it easy to remember the context of how you met.

Is my contact information secure?

Absolutely! We take privacy seriously. Your contact information stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Can I export my contact notes?

Yes, you can export your enhanced contact information including notes and tags to various formats for backup or sharing purposes.
