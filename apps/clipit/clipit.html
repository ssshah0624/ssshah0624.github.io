Frequently Asked Questions

How do I clip a moment?

Just tap 'Clip It' on your Apple Watch or phone after a great play. The app automatically saves the moments leading up to your tap.

How long does Clip It record before I tap?

Clip It saves the last several seconds leading up to your tap, ensuring you capture the full highlight. You can adjust this duration in the app settings.

Can I edit my clips?

Yes! After capturing a moment, you can trim, merge, and add effects to your clips before sharing.

How do I share my clips?

You can share clips directly to social media platforms, send them via messaging apps, or save them to your device's camera roll.
