Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track a feeding session?

Simply select your feeding method (breast or bottle) and tap "Start Feeding". The app automatically tracks duration and timing. For bottle feeding, enter the amount when finished.

Can multiple caregivers use the app?

Yes! Share access with partners, nannies, or family members so everyone stays updated on your baby's care routine.

How do I share data with my doctor?

Go to the Reports section, select your date range, and tap "Generate PDF". You'll get a professional report with all tracked data ready to share.

Does it work in dark mode?

Absolutely! We have a fully supported dark mode perfect for those nighttime feeding sessions without disturbing your baby.
