Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new trip?

To create a new trip, go to the "My Trips" section and click the "Add New Trip" button. Fill in the trip details and save.

How can I collaborate with others?

Planning group trips is easy! Share your itinerary with friends and family by clicking the "Share" button on your trip page. Everyone can view, add, and adjust plans together.

How does document storage work?

Simply go to the "Documents" section of your trip and click "Upload Document" to add files like tickets, reservations, or travel info. Access them anytime, even offline.

How does the packing list help?

Add items organized by category (clothes, toiletries, electronics) and check them off as you pack. Never leave anything behind again!
