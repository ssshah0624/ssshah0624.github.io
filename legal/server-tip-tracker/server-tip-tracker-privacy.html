Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 11, 2024

Server Tip Tracker (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is designed for tipped and hourly workers who want clear insight into their earnings. This Privacy Policy explains the data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to help you track and analyze your earnings:

  • Shift entries: Tips, hours worked, hourly wages, dates, and optional notes you enter into the app.
  • Employers and roles: Company names, locations, and job titles you add to organize your shifts.
  • Diagnostics: Aggregated, anonymized analytics and crash logs that help us understand app performance. These diagnostics do not include your shift details.

2. How We Use Information

  • Calculate earnings totals, averages, and trends across dates, weekdays, employers, and roles.
  • Display insights that help you schedule smarter shifts and understand how your income changes over time.
  • Provide customer support when you contact us.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Your shift data is stored locally on your device. We do not sell or rent your personal data.
  • If you choose to back up your device to iCloud or another service, that provider’s policies apply to the backup copy.
  • You can edit or delete your entries at any time from within the app.

4. Sharing and Third Parties

  • We do not sell or share your shift data with advertisers.
  • System providers such as Apple may process payments, diagnostics, and crash reports according to their own terms and privacy policies.

5. Security

We rely on Apple’s security model for local data storage. If you contact support, emails are transmitted over TLS. We regularly review logs to monitor app stability.

6. Your Choices

  • Edit or delete shift entries from within the app.
  • Export your data if you need a copy outside the app.
  • Request help or ask questions at admin@brightwoodapps.com.

7. Children’s Privacy

Server Tip Tracker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. We will update the “Last updated” date when changes are published.
